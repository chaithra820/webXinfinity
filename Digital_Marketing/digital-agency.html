Types of Digital Marketing :

1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) : It helps increasing traffic flow to website through organic research engine results. SEO is also the art and science of making search engines of Google Yahoo and Bing, to help reach content to their users. SEO also helps in the ranking of the website in search engines. Types of SEO On-Page SEO & Off-Page SEO.


2. Pay-per-Click : PPC is a type of marketing in which advertisers pay the search engine a small fee each time when their ads get clicked. Basically, it's buying visits to our sites to increase traffic flow, rather than waiting for organic flow. Major PPC Platforms are Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, Amazon Advertising. There are four benefits to PPC marketing Speed, Precision, Agility and Measurement.


3. Social Media Marketing :SMM is a type of marketing that uses social media and social networks to promote brands for their products or services. It's one of the trending ways of digital marketing, we can target an audience according to time, age, interest, etc. SMM also helps in keeps the public aware of all the new updates or upgrades in a shorter period. SMM is cost-effective comparatively.


4. Content Marketing : It’s a form of digital marketing focuses on creating, publishing, and distributing content for a targeted audience online. Content marketing attracts new customers by creating and sharing free content. It helps companies to build brand loyalty within customers, provides valuable information to consumers, and creates an urge to purchase products from the company. Written, audio, video and images are the forms of content marketing.


5. Email Marketing :It’s the very first form of digital marketing that was started in 1971. Based on sending a commercial message to a group of people, using email is known as email marketing. It helps customers on companies’ email lists to be aware of new products, discounts, and other services. Business use Email Service Provider (ESP) to send marketing emails and it also helps with managing email marketing campaigns.


6. Mobile Marketing :In recent years, people have shifted their attention (and dollars) to mobile. Utilizing this advantage marketers campaign using SMS/MMS and mobile applications. Mobile marketing helps in building any kind of long-term and short-term marketing plan. These days mobile phones are more than people in the world, this makes mobile a powerful tool for marketing purpose.


7. Marketing Agency :Marketing agencies helps clients with pinpointing marketing strategies that will drive the best business resulting and maximising return on investment.It’s a business responsible for research, analysis, strategy, branding and promotion of products or services.


8. Affiliate Marketing: It’s an advertising model in which a third-party publisher is generated to increase traffic flow or leads to the company’s products and services. That third-party publishers are affiliates. Revenue is shared between online advertisers and online publishers, which’s based on performance typically in the form of sales, registration, or clicks.
